About

My expertise as a videographer goes beyond simply pointing and shooting—I'm a dedicated visual storyteller. I specialize in two very different, yet equally compelling, forms of filmmaking: capturing the raw emotion of weddings and proposals and creating the stylized, high-energy world of music videos. For weddings and proposals, my focus is on preserving authentic moments. I work discreetly to capture the heartfelt vows, the spontaneous laughter, and the intimate glances that make your story unique. The goal is to create a timeless film that not only documents the day but allows you to relive the powerful emotions for years to come. With music videos, the process is all about creative vision and collaboration. I partner with artists to bring their sound to life, using dynamic visuals, strategic shot planning, and precise editing to create a compelling narrative. Whether it's a high-energy performance or a mood-driven visual, I craft a film that amplifies the music and leaves a lasting impression. By working across both of these worlds, I bring a versatile skill set to every project—the documentary-style authenticity of a wedding filmmaker and the creative polish of a music video director
